Privacy Policy - Cellarmaker Sales Tracker

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Cellarmaker Sales Tracker is an internal business tool for Cellarmaker Brewing Company employees to track sales, manage customer accounts, and calculate commissions.

Information We Collect

  • Employee names and login credentials
  • Sales and commission data synced from Cin7 inventory system
  • Customer account information (names, contact details, purchase history)
  • Sales lead data from Untappd
  • User activity within the app

How We Use Information

  • Track sales performance and generate reports
  • Calculate employee commissions
  • Manage customer accounts and relationships
  • Generate and track sales leads
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is securely stored on Railway PostgreSQL database
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Access is restricted to authorized Cellarmaker employees only
  • Authentication required via username and password

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • Cin7: Inventory management and sales data synchronization
  • Railway: Secure cloud hosting and database services
  • Untappd: Sales lead generation (public venue data only)

Data Retention

We retain sales and commission data for business and accounting purposes as required by law. Employee accounts remain active during employment and are deactivated upon termination.

Your Rights

As an employee using this app, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request account deactivation upon employment termination

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated to all users.
